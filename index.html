Our Heritage

Crafting trust since 1983

For over four generations, New Latest Tailors has been synonymous with bespoke custom tailoring. Located in Udaipur, Rajasthan, we have served thousands of patrons who value precision, comfort, and timeless fashion. Whether it is an intricate bridal blouse with heavy hand-embroidery, or a structured gents tuxedo stitched to absolute perfection, our masters ensure every garment is a masterpiece.
